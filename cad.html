SUMMARY

Summary

总结

This tutorial demonstrates the workflow of custom automotive part design leveraging modern CAD software and AI assistants. Key learning objectives are listed as follows:

  1. Export 3D models from the online platform SophiCar
  2. Process F3D format files in Fusion 360
  3. Conduct parametric design with Autodesk Assistant AI
  4. Write effective prompts containing precise dimensions and placement information
  5. Iterate design revisions (adjust seat length, armrest thickness, etc.)
  6. Search and download models from Autodesk Community Gallery
  7. Model calibration and optimization inside Fusion 360
